Speeding above 105 km/h will incur a penalty of ₹1,000 per instance.

Extra kilometers beyond the limit will be charged at ₹13 per km.

4. Smoking

Smoking inside the vehicle is prohibited. A fine of ₹500 will be applied.

Strictly prohibited. A fine of ₹1,000 will be charged for violations.

If the vehicle is returned unclean, a fine of ₹500 will be imposed.

Charges of ₹5,000 will apply for damage to bonnet or car roof due to improper usage.

Dripping or liquid spillage inside the vehicle will result in a ₹5,000 fine.

If the vehicle is handed to another person without permission, a ₹5,000 penalty will be charged.

In case of total loss or major accident, the customer is liable to pay up to 45% of the Insured Declared Value (IDV).

Entry into restricted/banned areas without permission will result in a ₹10,000 fine. Customers must inform AlexaCars before changing routes. Geo-fencing is enabled to enforce this.

Customers must not drive a damaged car. Inform the company immediately in case of an accident. Continued use may lead to customer liability for repair costs.

14. External Branding

Any stickers, wraps, or branding not approved by AlexaCars are strictly prohibited. Violation incurs a ₹5,000 fine.

15. Spare Parts Handling

Customers must not change or remove any spare parts. Emergency cases require prior approval. Unauthorized changes will result in a ₹5,000 fine in addition to the part's cost.
